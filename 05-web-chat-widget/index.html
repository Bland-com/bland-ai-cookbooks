Features

Everything you need to grow

From first contact to closed deal, NovaCRM keeps your entire sales process organized and automated.

Contact Management

Organize all your contacts with custom fields, tags, and smart lists. Import from spreadsheets or sync from your existing tools.

Pipeline Tracking

Visualize your sales pipeline with drag-and-drop stages. See deal values, probabilities, and expected close dates at a glance.

Email Automation

Build multi-step email sequences that trigger automatically based on contact behavior, deal stage, or custom conditions.

AI Chat Support

Embed an AI-powered chat widget on your website that answers visitor questions, qualifies leads, and escalates to your team.

Custom Reporting

Build dashboards with the metrics that matter to your team. Track revenue, activity, conversion rates, and team performance.

API and Integrations

Connect NovaCRM to your stack with native integrations for Slack, Gmail, Zapier, and more. Or build custom workflows with our API.
